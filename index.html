About

I am currently completing my MSc in Computer Science at the Université du Québec à Chicoutimi (UQAC), Québec, Canada. My research sits at the intersection of trustworthy AI, software verification, and testing of intelligent systems.

I hold a Technical University Diploma in Computer Engineering and a Bachelor in Software Engineering from the Institut Universitaire de Technologie (IUT) Ngaoundéré (Cameroon), and a first MSc in Computer Engineering from the Pan African University Institute for Basic Science, Technology, and Innovation (PAUSTI), Nairobi, Kenya. During my studies I have completed multiple internships, published research, and built web and AI applications.

I am open to AI/ML engineering and research opportunities in Canada starting 2027.

Research Interests

  • Verification & validation of AI/ML systems
  • Formal verification and specification of system requirements
  • Software testing methodologies and quality assurance
  • Deep learning for computer vision and object detection
  • Trustworthiness properties of machine learning models
